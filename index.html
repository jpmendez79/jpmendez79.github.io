About Jesse Mendez

I am a second year graduate student at Louisiana State University in Baton Rouge, LA. My research interests are particle and neutrino physics. Currently I am working with Dr. Hanyu Wei studying neutrino oscillation.

I received my Bachelor of Science in Physics from California Polytechnic University, Humboldt in 2022. As an undergraduate student I worked with Dr. Charles Hoyle in the Cal Poly Humboldt Gravitational Physics Laboratory.

About this Website

This website contains the latest news and blog posts collected from my research and my other related interests. You can expect to read about Emacs, programming, and thoughts on pedagogy and teaching in addition to posts on physics and particle physics.
